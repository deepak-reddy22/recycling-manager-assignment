## Crisis Management Prompt
Evaluate the candidate’s ability to handle:
- Production failures
- Workforce shortages
- Safety incidents

Scoring Rubric:
90–100: Excellent leadership under pressure  
70–89: Good response  
50–69: Average handling  
Below 50: Poor crisis response  

Return a score from 0–100.
